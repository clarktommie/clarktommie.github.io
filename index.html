About Me

I’m a data scientist who enjoys turning raw data into clear answers. I build end-to-end workflows that collect, clean, and analyze information, then share the results through dashboards and concise writeups.

Recent work includes deploying interactive apps on Modal and Streamlit, summarizing large text datasets with LLMs, and visualizing health-related risk factors to guide decision-making.
