USAA Fraud Detection & Semantic Library

Semantic monitoring of FRB press releases and CFPB complaints with embedding search, OpenAI summaries, and a Streamlit dashboard on Modal (DTSC 3602).

  • End-to-end ETL: scrape HTML/PDF releases and complaints, clean/standardize text, embed with OpenAI models, store in Supabase
  • RAG workflow: focus-term detection, semantic search over OpenAI embeddings, and OpenAI-driven summaries of relevant enforcement actions
  • Complaint overlays: visualize CFPB complaint clusters and temporal signals alongside regulatory context
  • Semantic library: save/retrieve curated topic collections for analyst briefings
